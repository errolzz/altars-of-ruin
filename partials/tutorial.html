Tutorial of Ruin

You are sick of watching evil swallow your home

Your vow to destroy as much of that evil as you can

Interface

The play menu is at the bottom of the screen

It looks like this:

Menu

Hero

Travel

The yellow line is your experience bar

The red line is your health bar

Town

Town is your main hub for game interactions

You also regenerate health faster in town

General Store

Buy new items from shopkeep Tull

Pawn Shop

Sell items you find and don't need

Forge

Smith Muro can increase the quality of items

Infirmary

Healer Lelka can heal you instantly for a price

Hero Screen

The hero screen shows you all the information about your character

Passive skills increase your damage when using a weapon of that type

If you stop using a certain weapon type the related skill will decay

You also have access to your bag and equipped items from this screen

Stats

Strength

Increases your damage

Vitality

Increases your life

Agility

Increases your chance to dodge

Travel

From here you can travel to any zone in the game instantly

The lower the zone on the list, the harder it is

You also travel back to town from this screen

Zone Screen

Once you arrive in a zone you will see a list of enemies

You can inspect each enemy and attack them from there

Combat

After attacking you do not have access to the main menu

The enemy life bar is at the top of the screen

Yours is at the bottom

Flee Button

If you find yourself in a fight you think you will lose you can attempt to flee the battle

Combat Skills

At level 6 you choose your first combat skill and your second at level 12

Combat skills will appear below your health bar

Leveling Up

When you level up you will get to distribute 5 stat points

At level 6 and 12 you will get to choose a combat skill

Dying

Death comes with a heavy cost

You will lose both gold and experience each time you die

You finished the tutorial!
