Skills

Your skills will make up the identity or class of your hero.

Passive Skills

Passive skills are tied to the type of weapon you choose to use. They simply add extra damage to your attacks.

There is no difference between the item types during combat, just pick the one you like best.

The different passive skills are: sword, axe, hammer, shortblade, and knuckles.

Combat Skills

Combat skills are usable while fighting. You pick your first skill at level 6, and your next at level 12.

There are nine combat skills for you to choose from:

Strike

Heightened sense of combat allows you to see hidden openings in your enemy's defense.

  • An extra attack during combat
  • Does the same damage as a normal attack
  • Usable every 3 seconds

Mend

A lifetime of injuries have given you a new level of familiarity with your wounds.

  • Heal yourself for 25% of your total health during combat
  • Usable once per fight

Cripple

If you know how the body works, you know how to make it stop working.

  • Slows your enemy's attack speed by 30%
  • Usable once per fight

Sap

Warriors know that to rob a man of strength you need to rob him of his will.

  • Decrease the strength of your opponent by 30%
  • Usable once per fight

Scramble

The faster you can fight, the faster your enemy can die.

  • Increases the rate of your attacks by 40%
  • Lasts for 6 seconds
  • Usable every 12 seconds

Stun

Even the most stalwart of foes can be knocked senseless if hit them hard enough.

  • Stun your opponent and stop them from attacking for 4 seconds
  • Usable every 14 seconds

Blind

Hard to fight if you cannot see.

  • Decrease your opponent's agility and weapon skill by 50%
  • Lasts 6 seconds
  • Usable every 12 seconds

Cleave

Armor isn't much good when it's falling to pieces during a fight.

  • Reduces the effectiveness of an opponent's armor by 30%
  • Usable once per fight

Dread

Your ghastly visage can put anyone into a panic.

  • Your enemy panics and stops attacking you
  • Your damage is reduced by 40%
  • Lasts 10 seconds
  • Usable once per fight
