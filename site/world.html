Altars of Ruin

Town

Town is your main hub for game interactions.
You also regenerate health faster in town.

General Store

You can buy new items from the store. New items will come up for sale every so often so check back each time to stop by town.

Pawn Shop

This is where you sell off any items you don't want. Make sure to sell your loot so it doesn't weigh you down while fighting!

Forge

The forge can take your non-unique items and upgrade their quality. This is a costly operation.

Infirmary

You can spend gold here to heal you up to full health instantly.

Travel

You can travel to different regions, zones, and back to town through the travel menu.

Regions

Regions are large areas of the world, each containing several zones. You can travel between regions in the travel screen, where you can see the regions approximate level ranges.

Zones

Zones are single areas populated with monsters. You can move between zones easily using the travel menu.

Monsters

Each zone holds a unique set of monsters just waiting to be killed.

Unique Monsters

Each zone has a few monsters that are a bit harder to find and fight than the rest. A full list of these monsters can be found in the "famed kills" section of the in game menu screen.

Monsters on this list have a higher chance of dropping good loot.
