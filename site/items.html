Items

There are many possible items for you to find on your way to greatness. And you will need them to slay your enemies.

Weapons

There are 5 types of weapons: swords, axes, hammers, shortblades, and knuckles.

There is no benefit to one type over another, chose whichever you like. But once you make a choice, it is better to stick with it. Otherwise you will have to train up your passive skill each time you switch weapons.

Armor

There are 4 armor slots: head, chest, hands, and feet.

There are no skills associated with armor so wear whatever you like!

Magic Items

Any non-unique item can be a magic item.
Higher level monsters can drop items with more potent effects.

  • Name Effects
  • of brawn Strength
  • of the guard Vitality
  • of the thief Agility
  • of haste Attack Speed
  • of quality Item Quality
  • of worth Item Value

Unique Items

Certain monsters within each zone have a chance to drop a unique item.
These items are generally better than normal items.

Each unique item can only be dropped by one or two monsters.

Good luck finding them!
