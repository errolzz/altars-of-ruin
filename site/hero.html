Altars of Ruin

Hero

You have the power to save this scorched realm!

Levels

There are 50 levels for your hero to play through. You advance in levels by slaying monsters to gain experience. You can see your experience bar in yellow at the bottom of the main game screen.

Each time you level up you will be able to distribute 5 stat points to strength, vitality, or agility.

Stats

Strength - Increases your damage.

Vitality - Increases your total life.

Agility - Increases your chance to dodge and decreases your enemies chance to dodge.

Equipped Items

Your hero can equip items on their head, chest, hands, feet, and hold a weapon.

The weapon you choose to equip will determine the passive skill you use.

Equipped items must be unequipped before they can be sold in the pawn shop.

Skills

Passive skills - Give you a damage bonus for specific weapon types. If you stop using one type of weapon, your skill in that item will go down.

Combat skills - Are skills that can be used during a fight. Each hero will select two combat skills, one at level 6 and the ext at level 12.
